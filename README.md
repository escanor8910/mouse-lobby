# 🏠 Mouse & Cat Lobby

A cute 3D **hangout lobby** you walk around in — on a computer or a phone!

- 🐭 Walk around and collect **pies** 🥧.
- 👕 Visit the **Mouse Shop** to buy fun mouse costumes (colors + a witch hat!).
- 🚪 Cross the door to the **Cat Home** — you turn into a **cat** 🐱 and can buy cat costumes.
- 🕳️ Step in a **vent** to zoom across the room.
- 🎮 Walk into a **game door** to launch **Cheese Dash** or **Sneaky Mouse**!

### Controls
- 💻 **Computer:** arrow keys / W A S D to move, press **E** to buy a costume or open a game door.
- 📱 **Phone/tablet:** joystick to move, tap the green **USE** button.

Made with [Three.js](https://threejs.org). It's all in one file: `index.html`.

> A polished web remake of the Codex *Mouse-vs-Cat lobby* (shop, costumes, vents, mouse/cat forms), turned into a hub for the two games.
